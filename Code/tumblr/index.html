About Me

I'm a third year PhD student in the Linguistics Department at Rutgers University. I received my BA in Liberal Arts from International Christian University, Tokyo, Japan. My passion is for the cognitive science of language. My research focuses on experimental and theoretical approaches to syntax, semantics, pragmatics and their interfaces.
